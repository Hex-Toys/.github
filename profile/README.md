## 🚀 What is HEX TOYS?

HEX TOYS is a purpose-built platform for:

- 🎮 Game collectors  
- 📦 Resellers (Whatnot, eBay, Vinted)  
- ⚡ High-volume live sellers  

It helps you **scan, price, manage, and export inventory at speed**, removing the friction from sourcing → listing → selling.

---

## ⚡ Core Features

### 🔍 Instant Price Intelligence
- Pulls **real sold prices** from marketplaces
- Aggregates data from:
  - eBay
  - Vinted
  - CeX
- Designed for **fast decision-making during sourcing or live shows**

---

### 📸 Smart Scanning & Lookup
- Barcode + image-based recognition
- Auto-fills:
  - Title
  - Platform
  - Metadata
  - Market pricing

👉 Turn piles of stock into structured inventory in minutes

---

### 📦 Inventory Management (Built for Resellers)
- Store items with:
  - Cost price
  - Estimated value
  - Notes / condition
- Track profitability across your stock

---

### 🔄 Cross-Platform Listing
- Export inventory to selling platforms
- Designed to integrate with:
  - Whatnot (live shows)
  - eBay
  - Vinted

👉 No more manual listing duplication

---

### 🎥 Built for Live Selling
HEX TOYS is optimised for:
- Fast-paced auction environments
- Bulk inventory prep
- “Random pull” / mystery formats

👉 Perfect for daily Whatnot streams
